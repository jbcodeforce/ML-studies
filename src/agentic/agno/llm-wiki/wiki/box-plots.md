# Box Plots

**Summary**: A simple graphical method for visualizing and identifying outliers in numerical data.

**Sources**: anomaly.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-18

Box plots are a graphical depiction of numerical data through their quantiles. They serve as a simple and effective way to visualize outliers within a dataset (source: anomaly.md).
