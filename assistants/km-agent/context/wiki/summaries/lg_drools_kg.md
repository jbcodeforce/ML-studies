# Agentic Solution with Decision Engines and Knowledge Graph

## Summary

This article explores the integration of agentic AI solutions with traditional decision engines (such as Drools) and knowledge graphs. The combination represents a hybrid approach where:

- **Decision engines** provide deterministic, rule-based reasoning for well-defined business logic
- **Knowledge graphs** offer structured semantic relationships and ontological reasoning
- **Agentic systems** contribute flexible, LLM-driven reasoning and orchestration

The intersection of these three technologies enables systems that combine the reliability of rule-based engines with the adaptability of generative AI, grounded in rich semantic context from knowledge graphs.
