# Summary: AI Players to Consider

This document catalogs notable AI companies and platforms across multiple domains — LLMs, inference infrastructure, vertical AI applications, and AI governance.

## Key Players

**LLM & Model Providers:**
- **Anthropic** — Claude 2 LLM with 100k context window, strong instruction following and logical reasoning.
- **Mistral** — Open-weight models and platforms, focused on developer adoption.
- **Fireworks.ai** — Inference platform with LoRA fine-tuning, custom FireAttention CUDA kernels delivering 4x speed improvements over vLLM for Mixtral-style models using FP8 quantization.

**Education & Training:**
- **Deeplearning.ai** — Andrew Ng's platform for deep learning education.
- **Kira Learning** — K-12 computer science and AI education.

**Vertical AI Applications:**
- **WorkHelix** — GenAI opportunity assessment scanning 450M+ data points across 70,000 work activities.
- **Bearing.ai** — Deep learning for maritime CII compliance prediction and fuel optimization.
- **Amorai** — Gen Z mental health coaching via LLM-powered exercises.
- **Workera** — Enterprise team skill assessment and learning plans.
- **SpeechLab** — Multi-speaker, multi-language TTS, speech recognition, and translation.
- **Esteam.ai** — Children's reading assessment and tutoring.
- **Netail.ai** — Retail and CPG AI solutions.
- **ValidMind** — AI model risk management.
- **Woebot Health** — AI-powered mental health interventions for specific patient populations.
- **Echelon.ai** — Real-time drone collaboration with AI-powered surveillance, search/rescue, and autonomous flight.
- **Landing.ai** — Visual prompting for computer vision.

**AI Governance & Tools:**
- **Credo.ai** — Intelligence layer for tracking, assessing, and managing AI systems for compliance and safety.
- **Outsystems** — Low-code platform with new AI agent builder.

This resource serves as a landscape scan of the AI ecosystem as of 2026.
