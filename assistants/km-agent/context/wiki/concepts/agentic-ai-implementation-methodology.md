# Agentic AI Implementation Methodology

A structured methodology for implementing generative AI and agentic applications in enterprise environments, combining established practices from decision automation, design thinking, and agile development.

## Core Philosophy

The methodology emphasizes starting with business problems rather than technology, focusing on where AI can augment human decision-making in business processes. Key principles include:

- **Business-first**: Always start by the business problem to solve
- **Augment humans, don't replace**: AI should help human actors in business processes, taking tasks quicker with better results
- **Start small**: Focus on day-to-day irritations for employees or customers before thinking big
- **Immediate value**: Narrow scope to deliver working software that proves value

## When to Bring AI into Business Processes

Generative AI is most valuable in business processes exhibiting these behavioral patterns:

- Humans are involved to make the process progressing
- The process is running for a long time
- Humans need extensive knowledge about the business, process, and historical cases
- Subject matter experts are leaving; new hires need efficient training
- Humans need help from SMEs on complex cases
- Humans need to consult other data sources for better decisions

## Methodology Requirements

The methodology must support:

1. Simple and short requirement discovery phase and use case selection
2. Focusing on tasks where AI can help human actors within a business process
3. Definition of a sustainable architecture with appropriate product and tool selection
4. Implementation of minimum viable product to prove value
5. Integration of continuous end-user feedback with solution playback and measurements
6. Data and AI integration across any cloud
7. AI bias measurement, secure and compliant deployment

## Best Practices Framework

- **Design thinking**: Empathy maps, pain/challenge identification, prototyping for early feedback
- **Lean startup**: MVP implementations, measure against hypothesis, pivot practices
- **Agile**: Test-driven development
- **GitOps**: Infrastructure as code for hybrid cloud deployment
- **Decisions and data focus**: Normalize decisions in context of business process, application, and data
