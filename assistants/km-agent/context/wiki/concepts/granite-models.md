# Granite Models

Granite is IBM's flagship series of LLM foundation models based on a decoder-only transformer architecture.

## Training Data

Granite language models are trained on trusted enterprise data spanning multiple domains:
- Internet content
- Academic materials
- Code
- Legal documents
- Financial data

## Data Filtering

Training datasets are rigorously filtered to remove:
- Hate, abuse, and profanity content
- Copyright and licensed materials
- Duplications
- Undesirable, blacklisted material and blocked URLs

## Capabilities

Granite models deliver strong performance on instruction-following tasks, including:
- Structured output generation
- List generation
- JSON output
- Instruction-based tasks

## Availability

Granite models are available through WatsonX.ai and can be used for inference, prompt tuning, and fine-tuning on proprietary data.
